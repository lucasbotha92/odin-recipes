Ramen Lasagna

Description

How to give an Italian a heart attack. A budget friendly lasagna that is still delicious and will fit most pockets.

Ingredients

  • 1kg Ground Beef or any meat of preference
  • 2 Big Onions
  • Vegetable Oil
  • 2tbs Garlic
  • 4 packs Instant Ramen
  • Tomato Paste
  • Italian Spices
  • Flour
  • Milk
  • Margarine
  • 500g Mascarpone Cheese/li>
  • 500g Cheddar Cheese grated
  • Salt
  • Pepper

Steps

Cook Meat

  1. On medium high heat, add oil to a pan
  2. Add onions and fry for 5 minutes
  3. Add tomato paste and garlic and fry for another 5 minutes
  4. Add ground meat and fry until the meat has some colour
  5. Add italian spice and salt and pepper to taste
  6. Lower heat and simmer for 45 minutes

Make Bechamel Sauce

  1. In a seaparate pot melt margarine and add flour to create a rough
  2. Add milk to create a bechemel sauce until sauce is the desired thickness
  3. Mix in Mascarpone cheese to the bechamel sauce

Bring Everything Together

  1. You will be layering everything in a backing tray. Start with meat, then blocks of ramen, then sauce. Repeat until everything is in the pan.
  2. Sprinkle the cheese over the baking pan
  3. Bake for 20 to 25 minutes in 180 degree Celsius oven.
