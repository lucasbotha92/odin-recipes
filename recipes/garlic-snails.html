Garlic Snails

Description

A delicious, creamy garlic sauce with snails served with crispy toast.

Ingredients

  • 200g Canned Snails
  • 1tbs Garlic
  • 2tbs butter/margarine
  • 250g Fresh Cream
  • 25g White Onion Soup Powder
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Milk
  • Bread of choice

Steps

  1. On medium high heat, melt margarine
  2. Add garlic and fry for 5 minutes
  3. Add snails and fry for another 5 minutes
  4. Add soup powder and stir, coating all the snails in the powder
  5. Add the cream, and salt and pepper to taste
  6. Bring to a simmer, then lower the heat and let the mixture simmer for 10 minutes
  7. If sauce is to thick, add milk to thin it out
  8. Toast your bread and serve
